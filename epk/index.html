Bio

Echoplex fuses nu metal, industrial, '90s alternative, and modern metal into a sound that feels both familiar and forward-leaning. Their music reflects a world caught in recursive collapse—externally fractured, internally conflicted—while exploring the repeating cycles within society, humanity, and the self.

Through Echoplex and their creative hub, Codex Collective, the project pursues evolution through sound—confronting chaos, extracting meaning, and moving toward ascension both musically and philosophically. The result is a body of work that sits at the intersection of analog instinct and digital consciousness.

All lyrics written by Sammy Mata and Rob Hoyt. All music written, performed, and recorded by Echoplex.
